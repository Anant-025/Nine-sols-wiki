Nine Sols

➤ Nine Sols is a lore-rich hand-drawn 2D action platformer developed by Red Candle Games. Set in the Taopunk world of New Kunlun, the story follows Yi as he uncovers the truth behind the Tianhuo plague, the rise of the Nine Sols, and the fate of the Solarian race.

Lore of Nine Sols

❖ What are Solarians?

Solarians are an advanced race native to the planet Penglai. For centuries they developed powerful technology and built a prosperous civilization. Their history was deeply shaped by both scientific progress and Taoist philosophy.

❖ What is Penglai?

Penglai is the homeworld of the Solarians. It was once a thriving planet powered by technological innovation before the Tianhuo catastrophe threatened the survival of the entire species.

❖ What are the Primordial Roots?

The Primordial Roots are ancient plants native to Penglai. They possess the ability to absorb sunlight and store it as Rhizomatic Energy through an unknown process. Their power became the foundation of many technologies and eventually the energy source of New Kunlun itself.

❖ Who was Lear?

Lear was the leader of the Fangshi Guild. The guild was formed to stop a tyrannical warlord from conquering Penglai through military force. After defeating the threat, Lear witnessed his own allies becoming corrupted by power.

Realizing he had gained the same level of control as the dictator he defeated, Lear disbanded the guild, sealed away dangerous technology, and hid the Rhizomatic Bomb. His actions encouraged Solarian society to abandon advanced science and focus on spirituality and Taoism.

❖ What is Tianhuo?

Tianhuo is a devastating virus that affects every Solarian. Victims gradually succumb to a disease resembling incurable cancer and eventually transform into white flowers upon death.

Although the public believed Tianhuo was a natural phenomenon, it was secretly created during Eigong's research into immortality and the Primordial Roots.

❖ Who is Eigong?

Eigong led a secret scientific council dedicated to restoring forbidden technology. While researching immortality she accidentally accelerated the spread of Tianhuo, causing a global catastrophe.

Rather than reveal the truth, the council presented itself as humanity's savior and promised a solution to the crisis. This allowed them to seize political power and begin work on a new future for the Solarian race.

❖ What is New Kunlun?

New Kunlun is a colossal spacefaring sanctuary built from the sacred island of Kunlun. Powered by the Primordial Roots, it was created as part of the Eternal Cauldron Project to preserve the Solarian race after Tianhuo spread across Penglai.

After leaving Penglai, New Kunlun traveled to the Blue Planet and remained there for centuries while gathering solar energy.

❖ Who is Yi?

Yi is the protagonist of Nine Sols. Once a trusted ally of Eigong, he helped develop many of the technologies that would eventually become part of New Kunlun.

Shortly before the launch of the Eternal Cauldron Project, Yi discovered that Eigong herself was responsible for the Tianhuo disaster and that the council had manipulated the crisis to gain power.

Horrified by the deception and the fate awaiting his sister, Yi confronted Eigong. He was defeated and left for dead, only to awaken centuries later aboard New Kunlun.

❖ The Beginning of the Journey

Upon awakening after five hundred years, Yi discovers a world transformed by the consequences of the council's actions. Seeking answers, justice, and redemption, he begins his quest to confront the Nine Sols and uncover the truth behind New Kunlun.
